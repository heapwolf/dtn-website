data terra nullius


Berlin, Germany - May 23 & 24, 2015


Terra nullius is a Latin expression meaning "land belonging to no one". In this context we are describing systems and protocols without centralized ownership.

What to Expect

Expect subversive and unconventional ideas that will challenge your thinking. Ideas less focused on implementations. A small event designed to promote constructive discussions between everyone involved. Expect a group of people who share an interest in securing the freedom of information and how we build systems around it.

What's Included

This is a not-for-profit event. All sponsorship pays for the conference itself. Aside from discussions and talks, attendees can expect home cooked traditional German food and incredible coffee from The Barn Rostery. All talks and interviews will be recorded and published online after the event.
