data terra nemo


Berlin, Germany May 23 & 24, 2015


register

Terra nemo is a Latin expression meaning "No man's land". In this context we describe systems and protocols without centralized ownership and how they impact the landscape of the internet.

What to Expect

Expect subversive and unconventional ideas that will challenge your thinking. Ideas less focused on implementations. A small event designed to promote constructive discussions between everyone involved. Expect a group of people who share an interest in securing the freedom of information and how we build systems around it.

What’s Included

This is a not-for-profit event. All sponsorship pays for the conference itself. Aside from discussions and talks, attendees can expect delicious food and incredible coffee from The Barn Roastery. All talks and interviews will be recorded and published online after the event.
